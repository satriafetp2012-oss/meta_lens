📖 About MetaLens

MetaLens is a free, browser-based meta-analysis studio developed for researchers and students in health sciences. It supports the full meta-analysis workflow — from data import and effect size conversion to pooling, heterogeneity testing, publication bias assessment, subgroup analysis, and meta-regression.

The tool handles four study types in a single workspace: Effectiveness (RCTs and interventions), Diagnostic accuracy (sensitivity/specificity/SROC), Prevalence (Freeman-Tukey/logit transforms), and Association (observational correlations and ratios).

All computations run entirely in your browser — no data is ever sent to any server. Your study data stays on your device.

🔒 Privacy & Data

All study data you enter is stored only in your browser's localStorage — it never leaves your device. No analytics or tracking are added by this app.
